My name is Japanese Learning Bot. I am here to improve your Japanese language skills.

Bot Commands:
/start - Start the bot
/settings - Customize your experience
/newwords - Learn new words that will be shown in the exam
/exam - Test with words that bot have shown you
/help - Show command list
/getinfo - Display information about the word you are interested in
/cancel - Cancel current action
/stop - Stop the bot and clear your information !!! Do not use this command if you aren't going to reset all your progress
